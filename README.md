## 📖 The Lore
You are a wandering Ronin in a land where hesitation is death. Your blade is fast, but are your eyes faster? Survive the staredown, ignore the enemy's shadow feints, and strike true. **Mushin (無心)** — *No Mind*. 

## ✨ Features
* **Three Paths of Difficulty:** Play as a Wanderer (Easy), Warrior (Medium), or face the brutal Demon (Hard) mode where the enemy draws in an unforgiving 200ms.
* **Procedural Audio Engine:** Zero external audio files. Sword slashes, tension rumbles, and success chimes are entirely synthesized in real-time using the Web Audio API.
* **Intense Visual "Juice":** Features visceral CSS screen shakes, blood-red flash overlays, and dynamic background Japanese Kanji that aggressively reveal themselves upon a successful strike.
* **Roguelike Achievement System:** Your path of honor is wiped clean upon death. Earn titles like *Sword Saint* and *Demon Slayer* in a single, flawless run.
* **Zero Dependencies:** Built entirely with Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JS. 

## 🎮 How to Play
1. Select your difficulty in the lobby.
2. Wait during the **Staredown (...)**.
3. If the enemy twitches with a **Feint (?)**, do *not* attack. Striking shadows brings dishonor and death.
4. When the red **Strike (!)** appears, click or press `Spacebar` as fast as humanly possible.
5. If you are slower than the enemy's draw time, you die.
